About the Author

Dr. Connor Robertson is an entrepreneur, author, and strategic advisor who has built his career around one principle: ownership creates wealth. Rather than following conventional career paths, Connor has focused on acquiring, building, and scaling businesses and assets that generate long-term value.

As the host of The Prospecting Show, Connor has interviewed hundreds of entrepreneurs, investors, and business leaders about the strategies that actually work for building wealth through ownership. These conversations, combined with his own experience acquiring and managing real assets, form the foundation of Buying Wealth.

Connor is the founder of Elixir Consulting Group, a strategic advisory firm that helps businesses grow through acquisition, partnership, and operational improvement. He also founded The Pittsburgh Wire, covering business and development news in the Pittsburgh region, and The Grant Finder, which connects organizations with funding opportunities.

His writing focuses on the intersection of ownership, leverage, and disciplined systems for growth. Buying Wealth distills these ideas into a practical framework that professionals and entrepreneurs can apply immediately.
