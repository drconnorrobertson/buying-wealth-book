What You'll Learn

The frameworks, strategies, and mindset shifts that separate wealth builders from everyone else.

🏢

Asset Acquisition Strategy

Learn how to identify, evaluate, and acquire income-producing assets across multiple categories. Stop building from zero when you can buy what already works.

⚖

The Leverage Framework

Understand how to use debt as a strategic tool, not a burden. Smart leverage accelerates growth when paired with discipline and proper risk management.

⚙

Systems for Growth

Build repeatable systems that let you evaluate deals fast, manage acquired assets, and compound your portfolio without burning out.

🧠

The Ownership Mindset

Shift your thinking from consumer to owner. Every dollar is either building someone else's wealth or yours. Learn to choose correctly every time.

Deal Evaluation

🔒

Risk Management

Wealth building is not reckless. Learn the guardrails, diversification strategies, and stress-test methods that protect your downside while maximizing upside.

About the Author

Dr. Connor Robertson

Entrepreneur, Author, Strategic Advisor

Dr. Connor Robertson is an entrepreneur, author, and strategic advisor who has built his career around one principle: ownership creates wealth. As the host of The Prospecting Show and founder of Elixir Consulting Group, The Pittsburgh Wire, and The Grant Finder, Connor brings real-world acquisition experience to everything he writes.

Buying Wealth distills years of hands-on deal-making, business building, and investment strategy into a single actionable framework that anyone can follow.
