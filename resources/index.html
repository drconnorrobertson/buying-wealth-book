Resources

Tools, worksheets, and links to put the Buying Wealth framework into practice.

Worksheets & Templates

10-Minute Deal Evaluation Scorecard

The five-question framework for quickly assessing any potential acquisition. Print it, keep it on your desk, and use it before committing time to any deal.

Due Diligence Checklist

A comprehensive checklist covering financial, operational, legal, and market due diligence. Designed to be adapted to any asset class.

Leverage Safety Calculator

Input your acquisition price, financing terms, and projected cash flow to determine whether your leverage ratio falls within safe parameters.

Post-Acquisition 90-Day Playbook

A week-by-week action plan for the first 90 days after closing an acquisition. Covers integration, quick wins, and risk mitigation steps.

Recommended Reading

The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham

The foundational text on value investing. Essential reading for understanding asset valuation and margin of safety.

Buy Then Build by Walker Deibel

A complementary perspective on acquiring small businesses as a path to entrepreneurship.

The E-Myth Revisited by Michael Gerber

Why systems, not heroic effort, are what make businesses work. Critical reading for post-acquisition operations.

Tax-Free Wealth by Tom Wheelwright

A deep dive into how tax strategy integrates with ownership and business structure.
